Our Menu

Chicken

Our signature roasted chicken is a culinary delight, meticulously prepared to achieve a perfect balance of crispy skin and juicy, tender meat. Each whole chicken is brined for a full day in a house-made mixture of sea salt, brown sugar, and a bouquet of fresh herbs, including rosemary and thyme, which infuses every fiber with a profound depth of flavor. We then slow-roast it in our custom oven, allowing the natural fats to render and the skin to caramelize into a delectable, golden-brown crust. This painstaking process ensures that the chicken remains incredibly moist and succulent from breast to thigh. Served on a bed of roasted root vegetables—carrots, potatoes, and parsnips—that have been caramelized in the chicken’s own juices, this dish is a harmonious blend of savory, earthy, and sweet notes. A final drizzle of a light, pan-jus reduction, created from the chicken's drippings, adds a final layer of richness that makes this classic dish an unforgettable experience.

Beef

Our beef dish is a tribute to rustic, slow-cooked elegance. We select only the finest cuts of boneless beef short ribs, known for their rich marbling and deep, beefy flavor. The ribs are first seared at a high temperature to lock in their juices and create a dark, flavorful crust. They are then gently braised for seven hours in a rich, aromatic liquid composed of dry red wine, beef stock, garlic, and fresh herbs like bay leaves and oregano. This long, slow cooking process transforms the tough collagen in the meat into gelatin, resulting in a fall-off-the-bone tenderness that is unparalleled. The braised beef is served atop a velvety smooth bed of garlic mashed potatoes, which perfectly soaks up the savory, concentrated sauce. To finish, the dish is garnished with finely chopped fresh parsley, adding a bright, peppery contrast that cuts through the richness and cleanses the palate. This is a dish of pure comfort and sophistication, perfect for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Sushi

Step into a world of pristine flavor and artistry with our sushi selection, where every piece is a testament to the purity of its ingredients and the skill of our master chefs. Our fish is sourced daily from sustainable, reputable suppliers, ensuring its unparalleled freshness and quality. We use only premium-grade, short-grain rice, which is meticulously seasoned with a delicate balance of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt to create the perfect foundation—slightly sweet, tangy, and firm to the bite. Our menu features a wide array of options, from classic nigiri and sashimi, which highlight the unadulterated taste of the fish, to creative rolls that marry traditional Japanese techniques with contemporary flavor profiles. For instance, our signature "Volcano Roll" combines spicy tuna and avocado, topped with a fiery, creamy sauce and a sprinkle of crunchy tempura flakes. Each piece is a miniature sculpture, carefully constructed to provide a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, offering a truly authentic and unforgettable sushi experience.
